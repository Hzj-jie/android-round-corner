-- Privacy Policy of Round Corner and More (org.gemini.round_corner) --

This application requires READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED and
SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW permission to perform its designed functionality, display
round corners (by default) or other user defined images (from storage) on top of
other windows after the android system booted.

This application does not collect or store or transfer any data for any purpose.
